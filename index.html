About Me

I am currently pursuing a Sustainable Systems Engineering degree with a concentration in Photography at Olin College of Engineering in Massachusetts. I am a student worker at the Office of Inclusion & Diversity, and working with the National Science Foundation & the National Association of Engineers to start a carbon-capture venture. I am also a part-time photographer at CultureHouse.

I have previously worked at Accenture, a Fortune 500 consulting firm; ALP Technologies, a clean-tech start-up; and Ai Robotics, a self-driving start-up. I have also served two years in the Singaporean Army.

I enjoy storytelling and communicating visually, and I find my photography skills valuable to my engineering work. In my free time, I can be found trying to focus my analog camera, or finding my favorite podcast (Top Contender: Skeptics' Guide to the Universe).
