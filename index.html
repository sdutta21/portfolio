Ironman themed Smart-Mirror

J.A.R.V.I.S

My goal was to build a smart mirror that I could glance at while getting ready for school. The main modules it needed was the time, weather, my calendar for the day, to-do list, and a news summary. I managed to use the magic-mirror module on GitHub and adapt it for my needs. Inspired by Marvel, I created my own JARVIS module which allowed the mirror to communicate simple messages such as ‘Good Morning’ using clips from the IRONMAN movies. The physical mirror was built using a recycled one-way LCD screen and a raspberry-pi with a Linux backend. I’m currently working on integrating it with WIFI speakers to use as an alternative to Google Home / Amazon Alexa in my dorm room.

Plastic Eating Enzyme

Emissions Cut Down Research

The goal for the team was to work towards cutting down on plastic emissions. We decided to approach the issue from the biological standpoint and wanted to create our own enzyme that could break down plastic. We started off by buying plastic disintegrating enzymes to compare our results to a bench mark. In order to create our own enzyme, we collected samples of plastics breaking down in the local disposal facility (Wellesley Recycling and Disposal Facility). We then set-up test tubes and cultures to let the plastic eating bacteria grow over several weeks. At the end of the study we compared the results of our enzyme with the ones we bought online, and in most cases it was either more or equally as successful in breaking down PHA (a biodegradable form of plastic). We plan on continuing this research to fine-tune our enzyme.

Ramen Maker

Our goal was to create a one-click ramen making solution that can be controlled by students remotely. We envision a world where students arrive to class not worrying about their first lecture of the day, but rather look forward to the smell of freshly made ramen.

Our ramen-maker has features such as a ramen-dispenser, a powder dispenser, a water dispenser, automatic control to turn on the hot-plate, and one-click start/stop functionality that has been curated to make the best type of ramen possible.
