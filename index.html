About Me

I am pursuing a Systems Engineering degree with a concentration in Sustainability and Photography at the Olin College of Engineering in Massachusetts. I am a student worker at the Office of Inclusion & Diversity, and working with the National Science Foundation & the National Association of Engineers to start an agriculture-tech venture. I am currently working at Lunar Energy as a Systems Integration Engineering Intern.

I have previously worked at Accenture, a Fortune 500 consulting firm; ALP Technologies, a clean-tech start-up; and Ai Robotics, a self-driving start-up. I have also served two years in the Singaporean Army.

I am storyteller and communicating visually, and I find my photography skills valuable to my engineering work.
